About us:

Welcome to JISMUN – Aljazari International School’s Model United Nations, where the leaders of tomorrow come together to engage, debate, and collaborate on global issues.

At JISMUN, we strive to provide a dynamic platform for students to step into the shoes of diplomats, representing nations in discussions about the world’s most pressing challenges. With a focus on fostering critical thinking, diplomacy, and teamwork, JISMUN equips participants with the skills they need to navigate an increasingly interconnected world.

Held annually at Aljazari International School, JISMUN brings together bright minds from diverse backgrounds to experience the thrill of international diplomacy. From tackling climate change to addressing human rights, our committees simulate the real-world workings of the United Nations, encouraging delegates to find innovative solutions through collaboration and negotiation.

JISMUN isn’t just an event – it’s a transformative journey. Whether you’re a first-time delegate or a seasoned MUN participant, JISMUN offers a supportive environment where every voice is heard and valued.

Join us at JISMUN, where ideas meet action, and passion meets purpose. Together, let’s shape the future, one debate at a time.
